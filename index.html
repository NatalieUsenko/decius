About us
Our story

Starting in 2011 out of Illia Pinchuk, the developer passionate about technology, DICEUS has become a reliable partner for custom software development and staff augmentation.

The company’s headquarters opened in Kyiv in 2012 when the team grew while working on an e-commerce project. Within ten years, we expanded our expertise and introduced our services in the USA, UAE, Lithuania, Denmark, KSA.

We strived to create a service that would be different from those available on the market all these years. Our goal was to guarantee customers that their software will be delivered on time, scope, and budget. Teamed up with like-minded people, we managed to do that. Today, we can confidently say that every project we accomplish is a success.

2021
DICEUS has gained a track record of 100+ projects and is trusted by more than 60 clients globally.
